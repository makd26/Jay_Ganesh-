Makarand Dongare, a very honest and caring human is the soul reason for kick starting this idea into a project. We are currently a team of 4 members - Makarand, Aviraj, Sanket and Pankaj.

Briefing-

India is a country of festivals,culture and traditions. Amidst this, there is a very famous festival celebrated with great fun and enthusiasm wiz. Ganesh Chaturthi. Anyone can find the thrill of Ganesh Chaturthi all over Maharashtra. One can kind various faces which will potray the "Unity in Diversity" of India. All over the Maharashtra one can easily get through various Ganesh idol and some stories behind the same. This Ganesh Chaturthi festive lasts for 11days.


Ideology-

People from different places and even foreigners visit various Ganesh Mandals during this period. They are completely new to this place. The crowd is large comparatively. So, they take help from locality people or keep someone as a tourist guide for them. Since, number of tourist guide are less, so most of them end up exploring out everything by themselves. To reduce and ease up their process of taking all the efforts by themselves, we the "Groundbreakers" have started working on this project. "Groundbreakers" is because, we believe in rational mindset and "Unity in Diversity" concept. Because we believe in exploring the best on India with the use of technology. Because we believe in the youths of India and the non stop development in the sectors of engineering and latest technologies. This site will basically provide all the details of small to big, famous to inconspicuous, large to timid and crowded to inadequate. Every mandal just have to register themselves and get connected to the whole world in the tip of their fingers. This site is the revolution for Ganesh Chaturthi festival.
